Ocean's 11 Movies

The Movies

Ocean's 11

“Ocean's Eleven” is a remake of the 1960 movie of the same name. It was released in December 7, 2001, directed by Steven Soderbergh, and distributed by Warner Bros.

In this movie, Danny Ocean plots a heist against his ex-wife's current boyfriend, and he successfully steals $160 million with the the help of the other 10 members of his crew

Ocean's 12

“Ocean's Twelve” was directed by Steven Soderbergh, released in December 8, 2004, and distributed by the Warner Bros.

In this movie, Danny Ocean's crew has blown through their share of the $160 million dollars, but their victim, Terry Benedict, has caught on, and demands that they return his money, with interest. Under the looming threat of 20 years in European prison, the crew scurry to return their money

Ocean's 13

“Ocean's Thirteen” was directed by Steven Soderbergh, released in June 8, 2007, and distributed by the Warner Bros.

Danny Ocean and his friends are out for blood after their friend is double crossed by ruthless casino owner Willy Banks.
